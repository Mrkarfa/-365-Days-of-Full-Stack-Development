About Greenwood University

Greenwood University is a premier institution of higher learning located in the heart of Springfield Valley. Founded in 1965, our university has been committed to fostering academic excellence, innovation, and community engagement for over five decades.

Our campus spans 250 acres of beautifully landscaped grounds, featuring state-of-the-art facilities, research centers, and recreational spaces. We are home to over 15,000 students from 50+ countries, creating a vibrant and diverse learning environment.

Our Mission

"To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values that prepare them to lead meaningful lives and contribute positively to society."

Core Values

Integrity - We uphold the highest ethical standards.
Excellence - We strive for academic and personal excellence.
Innovation - We embrace creative thinking and new ideas.
Diversity - We celebrate our differences and learn from each other.
Community - We foster a supportive and inclusive environment.

Campus Life

Life at Greenwood University extends far beyond the classroom. Our vibrant campus community offers countless opportunities for growth, connection, and discovery.

Student Organizations

With over 200+ student clubs and organizations, there's something for everyone at Greenwood. From academic societies to cultural groups, sports clubs to volunteer organizations, you'll find your community here.

Popular Organizations:
• Student Government Association (SGA)
• Debate Society
• Environmental Action Club
• International Students Association
• Entrepreneurship Club
• Drama and Theater Society

Athletics

Go Greenwood Griffins! Our athletic program competes in NCAA Division I across 18 varsity sports. Our facilities include a modern gymnasium, Olympic-sized swimming pool, and a 40,000-seat stadium.

Varsity Sports: Football, Basketball, Soccer, Swimming, Track & Field, Tennis, Volleyball, Baseball, Softball, and more.

Housing & Dining

Our campus features 12 residence halls offering various living arrangements, from traditional dorms to apartment-style housing. All first-year students are guaranteed on-campus housing.

Dining options include:
The Commons - All-you-can-eat dining hall
Café Verde - Coffee shop and quick bites
The Market - Convenience store and grab-and-go options
Griffin Grill - Sports bar and casual dining

Health & Wellness

Student well-being is our priority. The Student Health Center provides comprehensive medical services, while our Counseling Center offers mental health support. The Recreation Center features fitness facilities, group classes, and intramural sports.

Alumni Network

Once a Griffin, always a Griffin! Our global network of over 150,000 alumni spans across industries and continents, united by their Greenwood experience.

Distinguished Alumni

Dr. Michael Rodriguez, Class of 1985

Nobel Laureate in Medicine
Dr. Rodriguez's groundbreaking research in immunotherapy has transformed cancer treatment worldwide. He credits his Greenwood education for "fostering the curiosity that drives my research today."

Jennifer Park, Class of 1992

CEO, TechVision Inc.
A pioneer in the tech industry, Jennifer has led TechVision to become a Fortune 500 company. She actively mentors current Greenwood business students and funds entrepreneurship scholarships.

David Okonkwo, Class of 2005

Olympic Gold Medalist, Track & Field
David won three Olympic gold medals and remains active in youth athletics programs. The Okonkwo Sports Complex on campus bears his name.
